Karne Norte Guisado

Description

Karne Norte Guisado (Cornedbeef Guisado) is a popular dish in Philippines.It is easy to cook(beginner friendly) and tasty and affordable those are some of the the reasons why Filipinos love it.
It can be serve anytime in the day and it's best with rice or bread.
A notable variant of the dish is sinabawang("sabaw" means "broth") corned beef, which just adds beef stock or water to the dish after saut√©ing, making it soupier.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 large potato(peeled and diced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 can of cornedbeef
  • 1/2 cup beef stock(can also be made using beef bouillon/broth cubes)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a cooking pan over medium-low heat
  2. When the oil is hot enough, Saute the minced onion first(?) then add garlic
  3. Add cornedbeef then, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add potatoes and beef stock.

    5. (option): if you want it soupier make it 1 cup of broth instead of 1/2cup

  5. Stir and let it boil. Cover it with lid and cook for 6-8 minutes(depends on your preference on potato firmeness)
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve it. Enjoy!!
